Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026. This policy explains how 3DPrintMap collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

3DPrintMap is a public directory. We collect and display the following types of business information to help users find 3D printing services:

  • Business information: Business name, address, phone number, email address, website URL, and social media links submitted by business owners or sourced from publicly available data.
  • Usage data: When you visit 3DPrintMap, our hosting provider (Vercel) may automatically collect standard server log information including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time of visit. This data is used solely for site performance and security monitoring.
  • Contact form submissions: If you email us at support@3dprintmap.com to add or update a listing, we retain your email address and message to process your request.

We do not require users to create accounts, and we do not collect personal information from visitors browsing the directory.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected by 3DPrintMap is used only for the following purposes:

  • To display business listings in the 3DPrintMap directory so users can find 3D printing services near them.
  • To respond to business owner requests to add, update, or remove listings.
  • To monitor and improve site performance and security.
  • To generate anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., most-viewed cities or technologies).

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

3DPrintMap uses minimal cookies necessary for basic site functionality:

  • Session cookies: Used to remember your filter and search preferences during a browsing session. These expire when you close your browser.
  • Analytics: We may use privacy-respecting analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site in aggregate. No personally identifiable information is tracked.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not affect your ability to browse the directory.

Third-Party Services

3DPrintMap integrates with and links to third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these services:

  • Vercel: Our hosting provider. Vercel processes server requests and may log IP addresses for security. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: Used to load typography. Google may log font requests. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Maps: Business rating and review data sourced from Google Maps. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Business websites: Clicking a business's website link will take you to that business's own site, subject to their own privacy policy.

Your Rights

If your business is listed on 3DPrintMap and you would like to:

  • Update or correct your listing information
  • Remove your business from the directory
  • Request a copy of any data we hold about your business

Please email us at support@3dprintmap.com and we will process your request as soon as possible.

3DPrintMap is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us at support@3dprintmap.com.
