3D Printing in Seattle

Home to Boeing's commercial airplane division and a dense cluster of aerospace suppliers, Seattle has long been at the forefront of precision manufacturing. 3D printing has become essential to the region's aerospace supply chain — from tooling and jigs to flight-test components and interior parts.

Beyond aerospace, Seattle's tech giants — Amazon, Microsoft, and hundreds of startups — generate constant demand for rapid prototyping. The city's thriving makerspace community, anchored by spaces like Metrix Create:Space, gives individuals and small businesses access to professional-grade printing.

Industries We Serve in Seattle

Aerospace & Defense

Tooling, jigs, fixtures, and flight-test components for Boeing and the broader aerospace supply chain.

Technology & Software

Rapid prototyping for hardware teams at Amazon, Microsoft, and Seattle's dense startup ecosystem.

Marine & Maritime

Custom brackets, housings, and replacement parts for commercial vessels and recreational boating.

Healthcare & Biotech

Anatomical models, surgical guides, and lab equipment for the region's growing life sciences sector.

Education & Research

University of Washington research programs and K-12 makerspaces driving academic and STEM printing.

Consumer Products & Startups

Product development, packaging prototypes, and short-run manufacturing for consumer goods brands.
