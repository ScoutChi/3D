3D Printing in New York

New York's 3D printing services span the full spectrum — from boutique SLA shops in SoHo serving the fashion and jewelry industries to large FDM operations in Long Island City serving engineering firms and production companies.

The city's concentration of designers, architects, medical researchers, and film/TV production companies makes it one of the most active markets for on-demand fabrication in the US.

Industries We Serve in New York

Fashion & Jewelry

NYC's fashion industry uses high-resolution SLA and resin printing for custom accessories, runway pieces, and jewelry masters.

Architecture & Real Estate

Manhattan's top architecture firms produce detailed scale models and presentation pieces for development pitches and design reviews.

Film & Media Production

NYC's film, TV, and advertising industries rely on 3D printing for props, set pieces, and custom production hardware.

Engineering & Prototyping

Startups and engineering firms across the five boroughs use 3D printing to rapidly test concepts and develop functional prototypes.

Life Sciences

NYC's growing biotech sector and major hospitals use 3D printing for research models, surgical planning, and medical device development.

Consumer Products

Consumer goods companies headquartered in New York use local 3D printing for packaging prototypes, product development, and marketing samples.
