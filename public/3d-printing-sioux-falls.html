3D Printing in Sioux Falls

Home to two major health systems — Sanford Health and Avera — Sioux Falls is a significant medical hub for the Dakotas region. Its 3D printing shops serve medical professionals needing custom anatomical models, surgical guides, and prosthetic components.

Beyond healthcare, the city's growing manufacturing base and strong agricultural economy generate steady demand for custom tooling, replacement parts, and prototype development.

Industries We Serve in Sioux Falls

Healthcare & Medical

Sanford Health and Avera Health systems drive demand for anatomical models, surgical planning guides, and custom medical device components.

Agriculture & Farm Equipment

South Dakota's agricultural industry relies on 3D printing for custom equipment parts, precision tooling, and prototype development for farm machinery.

Financial Services Technology

Sioux Falls' major financial sector uses 3D printing for data center hardware prototypes, custom equipment enclosures, and technology product development.

Manufacturing & Industrial

The city's expanding manufacturing base uses additive manufacturing for jigs, fixtures, tooling aids, and low-volume production components.

Consumer Retail

Retail and consumer goods companies use 3D printing for display fixtures, promotional items, packaging prototypes, and custom product development.

Education & Research

The University of South Dakota and South Dakota State University generate research and educational prototyping demand across multiple disciplines.
