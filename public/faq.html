General

3DPrintMap is the most comprehensive online directory of 3D printing services across the United States. We help customers find local and national 3D printing companies by technology, material, service type, and location — all in one place, for free.

Listings are sourced from publicly available business data and verified against Google Maps. We check for accuracy of name, address, and contact details. If you spot an error, please email hello@3dprintmap.com and we'll correct it promptly.

We add new listings and update existing ones on an ongoing basis. New business submissions are processed within 48 hours. If you notice outdated information on any listing, let us know.

We list any business offering 3D printing services to the public or to other businesses — including FDM printing shops, SLA/resin studios, SLS service bureaus, metal printing facilities, and companies offering 3D design and CAD services.

Currently 3DPrintMap is focused on the United States. We plan to expand to Canada and other English-speaking markets in the future. If you operate internationally and want to be considered, email us.
