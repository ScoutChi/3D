3D Printing in Los Angeles

The greater LA area is home to a unique mix of creative and technical industries that drive high demand for 3D printing. The entertainment industry relies on custom props, prosthetics, and set pieces. Meanwhile, the South Bay aerospace and defense cluster is one of the densest in the US.

LA's fashion and product design industry — centered in the Arts District and downtown — uses SLA and multi-material printing for prototyping accessories, footwear, and packaging. Medical device companies in the Conejo Valley and Torrance round out the market with demand for precision, biocompatible parts.

Industries We Serve in Los Angeles

Film, TV & Entertainment

Custom props, prosthetics, costumes, and set pieces for Hollywood studios and independent productions.

Aerospace & Defense

Precision components and tooling for the South Bay cluster including SpaceX, Northrop Grumman, and Raytheon.

Fashion & Product Design

Accessory prototypes, footwear models, and packaging mockups for LA's thriving design industry.

Medical Devices

Biocompatible prototypes and surgical guides for medical device companies in the Conejo Valley and Torrance.

Architecture & Real Estate

Detailed scale models, site study prints, and conceptual massing for LA's active architecture market.

Automotive & EV

Rapid prototyping for interior components, exterior trim, and custom parts for LA's auto and EV sector.
