3D Printing in Portland

Portland is home to global footwear and apparel giants Nike (Beaverton), Adidas North America, and Columbia Sportswear — all of which use 3D printing extensively for rapid prototyping of shoes, gear, and accessories. This concentration of consumer product brands has created a strong local ecosystem of prototyping and fabrication services.

The city's thriving independent maker scene, centered in neighborhoods like the Pearl District and Southeast Portland, drives demand for small-batch production, custom parts, and art installations. Intel's large campus in Hillsboro adds semiconductor and electronics manufacturing demand just west of the city.

Industries We Serve in Portland

Footwear & Apparel Prototyping

Shoe lasts, sole prototypes, and wearable component mockups for Nike, Adidas, Columbia, and Oregon's footwear supply chain.

Technology & Semiconductor

Custom fixtures, test jigs, and enclosure prototypes for Intel's Hillsboro campus and Portland's tech company ecosystem.

Outdoor Recreation & Gear

Functional gear prototypes and custom components for Portland's outdoor industry brands and equipment designers.

Architecture & Design

Scale models, concept studies, and detailed architectural prints for Portland's active design and urban development community.

Creative & Art Installations

Large-format art pieces, sculptural components, and custom props for Portland's independent artist and maker community.

Medical & Dental

Dental aligners, surgical guides, and research models for Portland's healthcare providers and medical device companies.
