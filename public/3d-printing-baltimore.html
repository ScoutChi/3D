3D Printing in Baltimore

Home to Johns Hopkins Hospital — consistently ranked the top hospital in the US — and the University of Maryland Medical System, Baltimore is a major center for medical research and device development. Local 3D printing shops serve biomedical researchers, surgical teams, and medical device startups with anatomical models, surgical guides, and prototype devices.

The port of Baltimore drives demand from logistics, maritime, and import/export businesses, while defense contractors supporting nearby NSA, Fort Meade, and Aberdeen Proving Ground add government-sector work. The city's revitalized industrial areas are home to a growing community of makers and small manufacturers.

Industries We Serve in Baltimore

Medical & Biomedical Research

Defense & Government

Education & Research

Manufacturing & Industrial

Custom tooling, jigs, and production parts for Baltimore's revitalized industrial and light manufacturing sector.
